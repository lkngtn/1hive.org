    1Hive is made up of individuals and organizations that have contributed in various ways to advancing 1Hive’s cause.

    Often this simply means holding honey, participating in governance, interacting with other community members, engaging in discussion. Keep in mind that we are all 1Hive, and you can contribute to 1Hive regardless of skill or talent.

    However some individuals and organizations have made particularly noteworthy contributions that are worth highlighting more specifically:

    Blossom Labs

    Helped build and launch the 1Hive DAO protocol, and continues to work on various improvements to Gardens interface and Conviction. They also do freelance web3 development work.

    # Honeyswap Swarm

    an informal working group of individual contributors lead by paul2 maintaining and improving Honeyswap.

    # Buzz swarm

    an informal working group of individual contributors lead by zero8 maintaining the @1hiveorg twitter account.

    Quests swarm

    an informal working group of individual contributors lead by Gossman developing a bounty/escrow application integrated with Celeste.

    # Gardens swarm

    an informal working group of individual contributors maintaining and improving the 1Hive DAO protocol and Gardens interface. Lead by paul2 and Felipe Novaes Rocha

    Aragon

    Built aragonOS and Aragon Court, which are foundational technologies used in 1Hive DAO protocol. They also provided funding support through a grant for the initial development of Conviction.

    Commons Stack

    Contributed to the research and design of the 1Hive DAO protocol including in-depth modeling of Conviction. They also helped adapt the protocol and interface to support the Token Engineering Commons.

    Block Science

    is an engineering, R&D, and analytics firm specializing in complex systems. They wrote a paper on Conviction and provided feedback on its implementation. They also maintain cadCAD an open source tool that members of the community have used to model system dynamics of honey and Conviction.

    Gnosis

    maintains various Gnosis Chain infrastructure where the 1Hive DAO protocol is deployed. Also maintains Gnosis Safe which is currently used as a multisig by several 1Hive working groups, and which is likely be used as the basis for _DRAFT 1Hive Protocol 2.0

    # 1Hive LLC

    Helped build and launch the 1Hive DAO protocol, and currently maintains 1hive.org, The cult of 1Hive discord server, and forum.1hive.org
